Are you addicted to your cell phone?

Be honest...

You lock your front door and are about to head to a movie when you realize you left your phone inside. What do you do?



When’s the last time you checked a notification on your phone (besides a calendar appointment or text)?



Do you text and drive?



The first snow of the season is winding down outside, and the dog needs a walk. You’ve got your keys, dog leash, and winter gear. Anything else you need?



Have you ever heard a ping and thought you got a notification, only to check your phone and realize you’d imagined it?



Let’s say you’re out to lunch with a new acquaintance. At what point do you take out your phone?



Can you remember the last time you got absorbed in something for an hour or more without thinking about your phone?



When’s the last time you scrolled through something on your phone while a friend or loved one was talking to you?



Your phone broke, and you won’t get a replacement until tomorrow at the earliest. How do you feel?



You’re at home, about to fall asleep. Where’s your phone?



You are...
Straightedge

Kudos: you know how to protect yourself from addictive design patterns in modern tech. You may even have the most straightedge accessory of all: a dumb phone.

You are a...
User

Sure, you can get a little distracted by your phone, but it’s not like it impedes your ability to function. You could cut back anytime you want; you just don’t want to right now. Or maybe ever.

You are a...
Junkie

You’re the owner and the phone is your instrument, right? Of course, absolutely, and you’re prepared to defend yourself because—PING!—hold that thought, you’ve got to check your messages…
